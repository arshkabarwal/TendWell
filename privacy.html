Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

Overview

TendWell is built with your dog's wellbeing — and your privacy — in mind. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

Your dog's profile — name, breed, age, weight, sex, coat type, and physical traits you enter voluntarily.

Photos you take within the app when using the Health Scan feature.

Locally generated data — care event logs, scan records, and monthly recap summaries stored on your device via SwiftData.

How We Use Your Information

Health Scan photos are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for AI-powered visual analysis. Images are transmitted securely over HTTPS and are not retained by TendWell after the analysis is returned. Anthropic's own privacy policy governs how they handle data on their end.

Dog profile data is used solely to personalise your care schedule and is never uploaded to TendWell servers — it lives on your device only.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All app data (dog profiles, scan records, care logs, recaps) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. TendWell does not operate its own cloud database. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

TendWell uses the Anthropic Claude API to analyse photos submitted through the Health Scan feature. We encourage you to review Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

TendWell is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling TendWell. If you have questions about your data or this policy, contact us at arshkabarwal@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated. Continued use of TendWell after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
